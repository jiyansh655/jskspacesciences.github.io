SPACE SHUTTLE AND ITS PARTS





There are many space shuttles launched in the universe, but did you know how are they made? Today we are gonna show you how are space shuttles are made and how these parts works

space shuttle diagram




Capsule




A Capsule is a kind of container that carrys a astronaut to the final place of space mission. This module has all the the things that are needed by a astronaut
-weight of a Capsule = 28,800 kg (63,500 pounds)
-torque(a force that tends to cause rotation) = 6kN




Fuel Tanks

The fuel tanks are the containers of fuel that contains liquid oxygen and hydrogen. There are many containers of different capacity some of which contains liquid oxygen and some contains liquid hydrogen. These containers are of different capacity that holds the fuel like in PSLV these containers are of less cpacity as comparedb to GSLV.
-fuel tanks in PSLV
mass of largest container = 20t
maximum capacity of fuel = 18t


-fuel tanks in GSLV
mass of largest container = 30t
maximum capacity of fuel = 27t




Fairings

fairings are the coverings use to cover payloads

-mass of largest fairing = 0.17t
-separation force = 0.85kN




Aerodynamics

Aerodynamics are designed in that manner so that it can cut the air while moving against the gravity




Engine

Engines are the main part of the whole rocket. Because of it the rocket are produced that much thrust so that it can move upward

-mass = 3.5t
thrust = 1,176,798N
efficiency(exhaust velocity) = 240 ISP




RCS Thrusters

A set of small thrusters used for giving directions

-mass of a thruster = 0.05t

Parts of a satellite

A sattelite is a main component of space vehicle. The main parts of sattelites are:-
Solar pannels:- these pannels are used to get connected from anteena as they get opened and get sunlight.
Antenna:- antennas are used to produce the connections in the form of electromagnetic waves.
Framing cameras:- the cameras used to capture image of the objects in the universe.
Gamma ray and neutron detector:- used to detect the the elements made on other surfaces than earth.




Parts of a rover

-Main arts of a rove
earth communication antenna:- this antenna helps to communicate to the headquarters of satellite control and the communicatiom happens in the form of electromagnatic waves.
Relay antenna:- this is the ultra-high frequency antenna helps to provide information on earth much faster if the rover is planned to far planets. The speed of this antenna is 2 megabits per second.
Loop wheel of a rover:- it is a special designed wheel to sustain the nature of the surface.
Hazard detectors:- it detects the upcoming obstacle at the way of rover and alerts the rover to stop or go in different direction.
Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generator:- it is a neuclear battery that converts the heat energy into electricity.
